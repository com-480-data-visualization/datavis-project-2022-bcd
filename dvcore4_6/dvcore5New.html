Who is the director suitable for your movie investment?

From the dataset, we notice that the films released in recent years have had relatively high profits. Most movies have a profit of less than 0.2x109 USD. You can search for directors with film profits greater than 0.2x109 USD. The directors of these films are definitely the best in the industry, and you can choose them with confidence. There are also a few directors whose films have negative profits, and you can try to avoid these directors when selecting.

In this chart, we present you with a scatter plot, with a horizontal axis showing you the timeline, starting from 2000 to 2020, and a vertical axis as the movie's profit. Each director corresponds to a color in the chart. The colors correspond to the directors' earliest active time, more early more red, and more recent more blue. If you click on a point which symbolizes a film, all other movies from the same director will be highlighted in the plot, with a floating window showing you the director's portrait and current film poster.
