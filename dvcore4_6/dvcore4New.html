What is the content that suitable for your movie?

The upper two graph shows the words extracted from each film's description. Each word's font size and the degree of the thickness represent the profits of the films which include this word in their description. When you choose film scripts, looking for the scripts related to these words may help you increase your expected income.

From the worldwide side, we can easily see that the films with the words "world", "new", "threat"," team" and "destroy" will have more significant profit.

Within the USA, the films with the words "family", "new", "school"," home" and "friends" will have more significant profits.
