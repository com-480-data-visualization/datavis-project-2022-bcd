Which quarter is the most suitable time for a profitable movie to publish

From the dataset, we notice that the films published in summer and winter gained more profits in most years, and the movies released in recent years have had relatively high yields. So if you want to publish a film, you'd better publish it in these two seasons.

The publish date is different in each country, even for the same film. Therefore we choose to use the publish date in the USA as a uniform standard.

In this chart, we present you with a bar plot, with a horizontal axis showing you the timeline, starting from 2000 to 2020, and each year has four bars to represent four seasons. The vertical axis shows each season's movie profit. If you click on a bar, a floating window will show you this season's two highest profit film posters.
