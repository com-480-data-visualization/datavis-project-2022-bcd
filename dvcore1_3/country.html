Brief Suggestions for Your Investment

Countries with darker colors are at higher priorty for you to choose as filming places.

Intro of Visualization

In this chart, we present you with a world map. Each country's color show this country's average film profit worldwide and in the USA, in two charts respectively. The darker the color, the greater the profit. If you click on a country, a floating window will show you this country's highest profit film.

Analysis Based on the Visualization

Shown on above map, we notice that the films in North America, South America, Australia and China have higher profits. Movies published in the Middle East and the South East will have lower profits. Movies filmed in countries with large population like China and India, or high-income developed countries, such as USA are usually in blue, meaning comparatively high average movie income.

According to this results, one possible explanation for differences in the average profits which are related to level of income and population in corresponding countries is that, movies filmed in such countries usually have closer connection to local culture, which means larger portion of audience in that country, and resulting in higher income. And this point worths being taken into account when planning for your movie.
