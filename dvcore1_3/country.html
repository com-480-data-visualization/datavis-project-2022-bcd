Which country is suitable for your movie investment?

From the dataset, we notice that the films in North America, South America, Australia and China have higher profits. Movies published in the Middle East and the South East will have lower profits.

In this chart, we present you with a worldwide map. Each country's color show this country's average film profit worldwide and in the USA. The darker the color, the greater the profit. If you click on a country, a floating window will show you this country's highest profit film poster.
