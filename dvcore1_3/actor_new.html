Intro of Visualization

This chart presents you with a bar that shows each actor's average film profit. The actors are sorted by their profit. There is a draggable thumbnail under the main chart, and you can drag it from left to right. If you hover over a bar, a floating window will show you this actor's photo.

Analysis Based on the Visualization

From the dataset, we could easily find that the top profit actors are Dileep Rao, Eve, Joonas Suotamo, Pierre Coffin, Daisy Ridley, Nathalie Emmanuel, Anthony Daniels, Jonathan Groff and Mark Hamill. The films in which these actors have acted are on average in the top 500 in terms of profits, so you can have fun exploring and choose from them with confidence.

Of course there might be dark horses who do not currently appear in the front, and therefore we provide a relatively long candidate list for you to select.
