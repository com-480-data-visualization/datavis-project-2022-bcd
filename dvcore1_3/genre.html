Guide

Which genre is suitable for your movie investment?

From the dataset, we notice that the adventure films have the highest profits, animation and action films also have high profits. Sports, war, history and biography films have relative low profits, so you may want to avoid choosing these topics while selecting scripts.

In this chart, we present you with a parallel coordinator. The vertical axes represent films' average and all income and profits from worldwide and the USA. If you click on a line, a floating window will show you this line belongs to which genre and this genre's highest profit film poster.
