Guide

Brief Suggestions for Your Investment

Adventure films have earned the most while movies in sports, war, history and biography genres have relatively low profits. Besides adventurous movie, animation and action films are also worthy of investment.

Intro of Visualization

Vertical axes represent films' average and total income from worldwide and the USA. Profits are calculated based on the budget and income. If you hover over a line, a floating window will present you the genre and its highest-profit film. Meanwhile, data for the genre will be highlighted to offer a clearer view of this genre.

Analysis Based on the Visualization

We provide several possible ways to analyze the data, in order to get some inspirations on your movie investment.

  • We can roughly classify genre of movies into four classes, high gain both in USA and worldwide, high gain only in USA and worldwide respectively, and relatively low gain both in USA and worldwide. As an investor, you could base on your location and budgets to decide a best genre for your movie.
  • On providing both income and net profits, the differences caused by filming budget is exhibited in this chart. For example, the high-gained genre, adventure, highest in average USA income, doesn't turned out to be also the highest one in average USA profits for granted, meaning that budgets actually have played an important role for adventure film. In this case, it's suggested to invest on adventure movie only when you are considered to have sufficient capital.
  • Comparing the total and average income, it is possible to find the kinds of movies which might not be in the trend. However, in contrast to the relatively low total income, those kind of movies gains considerable profits on average, such as sci-fi and animation.
